The History of Browser Flash Games

Explore the rise, golden age, and legacy of the Flash gaming era that defined a generation of online players.

1996

The Birth of Flash

Macromedia releases Flash (FutureSplash Animator), a tool for creating animations, which later evolves to include interactive features suitable for games.

2000

Newgrounds Launches

The first major platform for user-generated Flash games debuts.

2001

Miniclip Emerges

Miniclip revolutionizes online casual gaming with a vast library of games.

2004

Armor Games Takes The Stage

A new publisher and free web gaming portal for many innovative titles.

2006

Kongregate Arrives

A platform combining games, achievements, and community features is born.

2007

Social Gaming Emerges

Facebook incorporates flash games into their site, encouraging users to play games where they interact with their friends.

2008

Tower Defense Craze

Titles like Bloons Tower Defense dominate the genre.
